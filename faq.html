Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about your shopping experience.

How does the Virtual Fitroom work?

Our Virtual Fitroom uses AI to map clothing onto a customized 3D model that matches your height and weight, giving you a realistic preview of how the item will fit.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn items with original tags. You can initiate a return easily through our Returns page.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries. Shipping rates and delivery times vary by location and are calculated at checkout.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can also use our "Track Order" page in the footer.
